What does it add?

The headline feature of this mod is the ability to combine multiple flowers into the same block. Keeping in style of the Pink Petals, they're a lot smaller and flatter than the normal small flowers, so I called them Tiny Flowers.

Tiny Flowers can be mixed with up to 4 flowers in the same block. With 17 flower types (and empty spaces) in 4 slots in 4 rotations, this leads to nearly half a million possible combinations.

Why make this?

I've been slowly expanding what kinds of mods I want to make. The next stage was to make a mod that adds something new. In this case, the something new was a series of small decorative blocks that expand on something already in the game.

With Pink Petals in the game, and Wildflowers and Leaf Litter coming in a future update, I wanted to see what it would take to make a mod that allows you to put multiple types of flowers in a single block. It took some time to understand what I was even creating, but the ideas came together into the mod you see now.

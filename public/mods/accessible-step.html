Accessible Step

Accessible Step is a mod that increases the step height of the player so they can walk up full blocks the same way they can do with slabs. This is intended as an accessibility tool, as the game's built-in auto jump has serious issues that cause player frustration.

Download from Modrinth or CurseForge.

Get the source code on GitHub.
Do not download from other websites. At best you'll get an out-of-date version. At worst you'll download malware. I only upload this mod to Modrinth and Curseforge.

There are also options to change the number of blocks the player can step up when walking, sneaking, or sprinting. For example, you could try:

  • Setting the walking and sprinting step heights to 1.5 blocks to walk over fences.
  • Setting the sneaking and walking step heights to 0.6 blocks (like vanilla) but increase the sprint height to 1.25 blocks (to allow smoother movement while sprinting).
  • Setting all three heights to 0 blocks so that you stub your toe on every carpet you encounter.
  • Setting all three heights to 10 blocks, while setting your scale bigger using commands. You're now a giant!

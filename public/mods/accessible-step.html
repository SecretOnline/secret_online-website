Accessible Step

Accessible Step is a mod that increases the step height of the player so they can walk up full blocks the same way they can do with slabs. This is intended as an accessibility tool, as the game's built-in auto jump has serious issues that cause player frustration.

Download from Modrinth or CurseForge.

Get the source code on GitHub.
Do not download from other websites. At best you'll get an out-of-date version. At worst you'll download malware. I only upload this mod to Modrinth and Curseforge.
